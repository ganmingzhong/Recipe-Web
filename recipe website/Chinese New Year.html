Chinese New Year Dish

Prosperity Toss Salad

Ingredient

Main Ingredient:

  • 300g sashimi-grade salmon, sliced
  • 300g sashimi-grade kingfish, sliced
  • 10 wonton wrappers, shredded and deep-fried
  • 2 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 carrots, peeled and julienned
  • 1 continental cucumber
  • 1 daikon, julienned
  • 6 young leaves Chinese cabbage, shredded
  • 1 large pink grapefruit, torn into small pieces
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander, leaves picked
  • 2 tbsp Japanese pickled ginger, drained and julienned
  • ½ cup lightly crushed roasted peanuts
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves, stems removed and julienned
  • 150ml plum sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 100ml lime juice
  • 1½ tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp peanut oil
  • ¼ tsp five spice powder

Instructions

Arrange the ingredients:

  1. Arrange all the salad ingredients in decorative piles on a large platter. You can just place the ingredients in piles, or arrange them to create a picture or message for the new year.
  2. For the dressing, combine the ingredients in a bowl and stir to combine well.
  3. Place multiple pairs of chopsticks (1 per person) around the platter and gather the attendees around the platter. Pour the dressing over the salad and have the attendees toss the salad high in the air to mix well.
